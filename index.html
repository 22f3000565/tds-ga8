Financial Formula: Free Cash Flow

Free Cash Flow to Firm (FCFF):

\[ FCFF = EBIT(1-t) + DA - CapEx - \Delta WC \]

Where:

  • EBIT = Earnings Before Interest and Taxes
  • t = Tax Rate
  • DA = Depreciation & Amortization
  • CapEx = Capital Expenditure
  • Î”WC = Change in Working Capital

Financial Formula: Return on Equity

Return on Equity (ROE):

\[ ROE = \frac{\text{Net Income}}{\text{Shareholders' Equity}} = \frac{NI}{E} \]

Dupont Analysis Expansion:

\[ ROE = \frac{\text{Net Income}}{\text{Sales}} \times \frac{\text{Sales}}{\text{Assets}} \times \frac{\text{Assets}}{\text{Equity}} \]

\[ ROE = \text{Profit Margin} \times \text{Asset Turnover} \times \text{Equity Multiplier} \]

Financial Formula: Earnings Per Share

Earnings Per Share (EPS):

\[ EPS = \frac{\text{Net Income} - \text{Preferred Dividends}}{\text{Weighted Average Shares Outstanding}} \]

Growth Rate Calculation:

\[ \text{EPS Growth Rate} = \frac{EPS_{t} - EPS_{t-1}}{EPS_{t-1}} \times 100\% \]

Example:

\[ \text{EPS Growth} = \frac{4.50 - 4.12}{4.12} \times 100\% = 9.22\% \]
